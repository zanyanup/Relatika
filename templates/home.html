About Relatika

This is a dating web app that matches users based on what they have written on their profiles. We use advanced LLM models to analyze thousands of profiles and find you the best matches. Say goodbye to superficial dating based solely on photos. At Relatika, we believe that beauty fades, but personality only grows.

How It Works

Profiles are matched based on what you write in them using AI or LLM. Relatika is your personal dating secretary that browses through thousands of profiles and shortlists only the best for you to check and match if interested.

  • The AI revolution has made it possible for machines to read unimaginable amounts of text data and make sense of it. Why not use it to our advantage and try to make matches that are compatible with each other?
  • All you have to do is write about yourself and what you are looking for and let AI do the magic to shortlist user profiles for you. Find like-minded people, make meaningful connections.
  • Don't let a question bank of fixed 100 questions that everyone is given and an ancient matchmaking algorithm decide matches for you. Write freely about yourself, your dreams, your wishes, your goals, what makes you happy, sad, anxious, etc., and who you think your ideal partner could be or what you see you both doing together in the world? Write freely and we will get the AI secretary called "Relatika" to work for you to find this person.
  • In a sea of superficial dating apps which are only based on looks, Relatika comes as a breath of fresh air. We match users based on their personalities. The text of profiles is used by our LLM to match people. The photos are initially blurred and only revealed upon matching and hence sticking to our principle of non-superficial dating.
